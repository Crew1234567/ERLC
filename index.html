About the Game

ER:LC is a Roblox roleplaying game set in Liberty County where you can take on a variety of roles, including Police Officer, Sheriff, Firefighter, EMS, Civilian, and Criminal. Drive realistic vehicles, respond to emergencies, and roleplay with others in a highly interactive world.
